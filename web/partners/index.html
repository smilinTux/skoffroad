Why off-road, why in-browser

HTML5 game content gets shared ~12× more than passive web content. A playable beats a banner ad every time.
Brands using gamified experiences see ~22% lifts in customer retention. Driving simulators are stickier than scroll feeds.
In-game billboards bypass 100% of ad blockers — they're rendered as textures inside WebGPU, not served from an ad network.
Off-road is the perfect canvas: trucks, tires, gear, beverages, tourism, fuel — every adjacent vertical has a natural product fit.
WebGPU + Rust means console-quality visuals in a 12 MB browser bundle. No app store. No install friction. Mobile-friendly.
Everything is trackable. Impressions, dwell, click-throughs, completion rates, livery selection — exported to your dashboard.
