# skoffroad for Brands — one-pager

> **A playable off-road sim that runs in any browser. We turn it into a marketing surface for your brand.**

---

## What it is

skoffroad is a physics-driven off-road driving game built in Rust on top of Bevy 0.18 + Avian3D 0.6, compiled to WebGPU. Players visit a URL, wait ~8 seconds, and are driving a Jeep-class vehicle over procedurally generated terrain with raycast suspension, weather, mud, and trail scatter. No app store. No download. Console-quality visuals in a ~12 MB browser bundle.

Live build: **play.skoffroad.skworld.io**
