# Brand Outreach — email templates + target list

## Outreach principles

1. **Lead with the playable demo link.** Words don't sell games — games sell games. Always: "Try it in 8 seconds: play.skoffroad.skworld.io".
2. **One-paragraph pitch.** Brand teams get 200+ cold pitches a week. Earn the second paragraph.
3. **Specific, not generic.** Reference their last campaign. Show you've done homework.
4. **Pricing in the first email.** Cuts 80% of "send me more info" replies that go nowhere.
5. **One CTA.** A 20-minute call. Not a meeting, not a deck review, not a Q&A.

## Outreach cadence

**Week 1:** Apply to P0 sponsorship programs (Smittybilt, ARB, YETI, BRCC). Free, takes 30 min total.

**Week 2:** Cold-email 10 P1 brands. Use Template A. Track response in a simple spreadsheet (sent / opened / replied / call booked / closed).

**Week 3:** Cold-email 10 more P1s. Follow up week-1 sends (one nudge, never two).

**Week 4:** Pitch 3 agencies (Template D). If you have 1 closed deal by week 4, raise prices 20% on the next round.

**Ongoing:** Pitch 10 brands a week. Maintain 30–40 active threads. Industry conversion is 1–3% cold to closed, 10–20% from warm referrals — so the funnel needs volume early.
